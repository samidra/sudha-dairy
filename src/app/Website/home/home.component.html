About Shahabad Dugdh Utpadak Sahakari Sangh

Established in the year 1997, under the aegis of Bihar State Milk Co-operative Federation, Shahabad Milk Union, covers the vast and resourceful region along the colossal river Sone. The SMU was end implementing agency for the Operation Flood Programme and undertakes Dairy Development activities on the Anand pattern within its areas of operation. The establishment of union was a revolutionary step in itself, as it aimed to change the lives of farmers in and around four districts of the historically important Shahabad region.

Our Mission

Shahabad Dugdh Utpadak Sahakari Sangh Ltd will provide more cost & benefits to producers maintaining self dependency and Union will turn into leading Union of Country maintaining quality at every level and economical & social development of producers.
